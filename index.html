Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about creating your custom coloring book.

What types of artwork work best?

+

Almost any type of children's artwork works great! We can transform drawings, paintings, sketches, and even digital art. The clearer the lines and shapes, the better the final result, but we work magic with even the messiest masterpieces.

How long does it take to create my book?

+

Our standard turnaround time is 2-5 business days from when we receive your artwork plus shipping. Digital books are delivered within 72 hours of your order.

Can I see a preview before you print?

+

Absolutely! Just remind me in the custom instructions section of the form when upload your artwork and I'll send you any requested pages/files before printing. You can request changes or adjustments at this stage. We want you to be 100% happy with the result.

What if I'm not satisfied with my order?

+

We offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely happy with your coloring book, we'll work with you to make it right or provide a full refund. Your happiness is our top priority.

How do you handle my child's artwork?

+

We take privacy very seriously. Your child's artwork is only used to create your custom coloring book and is never shared or used for any other purpose. We securely delete all files after your order is completed.
